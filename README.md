# Mentrex Issue Tracker & Support

Welcome to the official support portal for Mentrex. We are committed to providing you with the best experience and value your feedback in helping us improve our platform.

## How to Get Help

We offer two dedicated channels for support and feedback to ensure your inquiries are handled efficiently:

### 1. GitHub Issues (Technical & Features)
For reporting bugs, technical glitches, or suggesting new features, please utilize our GitHub Issues tracker.

*   **Best for:** Bug reports, feature requests, technical suggestions.
*   **Action:** [Open an Issue](https://github.com/mentrexai/mentrex-issues/issues)
*   **Guidelines:** Please provide detailed steps to reproduce bugs or clear descriptions for feature requests to help us assist you faster.

### 2. Email Support (General & Account)
For private inquiries, account management, or billing questions, please reach out to our support team directly.

*   **Best for:** Account issues, billing, privacy concerns, general questions.
*   **Email:** support@mentrex.shop

## Response Time

Our team is dedicated to reviewing and resolving inquiries promptly.

*   **Standard Response Time:** You can expect a reply within **24 to 48 hours**.
*   **Urgent Matters:** We strive to address critical issues as early as possible.
